Hi there 👋! My name is Abigail Baidoo. I am currently a **PhD Candidate in Data Science** at the University of Cape Coast, supervised by Prof. Olivier Menoukeu Pamen, Prof. Nathaniel Howard, and Dr. Viani Biatat Djeundje. I am also an **Assistant Lecturer** at Takoradi Technical University.

My research focuses on mathematical modelling in microfinance, with particular emphasis on:
- 📊 Probability of Default prediction
- 💰 Credit risk rating for Microfinance Institutions
- 📈 Mathematical models for lending

I hold an **MPhil in Mathematics** from the University of Cape Coast (2019), an **MSc in Mathematical Sciences** from the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) - Ghana (2018), and a **BEd in Social Sciences (Economics and Mathematics)** from the University of Cape Coast (2016).

Beyond research, I'm passionate about teaching 👩🏿‍🏫 and mentoring the next generation of African mathematicians and data scientists. I'm an active member of the AIMS community and have participated in numerous workshops and conferences across Africa and Europe.
